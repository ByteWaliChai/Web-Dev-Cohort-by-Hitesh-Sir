About Us

UrbanGlow is a unisex salon in Patna, Bihar. We offer clean, professional grooming and beauty services at transparent prices. With experienced stylists and a commitment to hygiene, we serve both men and women with equal care.

Our goal is to remove the confusion customers face when trying to find reliable salons — no hidden costs, no waiting guesswork, and clear communication.
